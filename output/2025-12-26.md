**Method:**

**Data:**

**Climate Relevance:**
Directly addresses a critical assumption in climate impact assessments: that finer spatial resolution automatically improves projection reliability for policy-relevant decisions on adaptation and mitigation planning.

**Implications:**

**Limitations:**

**TL;DR:**
